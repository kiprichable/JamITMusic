A brief Bootstrap primer

Bootstrap has literally thousands of features for typography, forms, modals, and more. All of which are available through this template for UMD. The key is to keep it consistent and to keep it accessible. Below is a small sample of some of the options available to you, with more available at GetBootstrap.com

Headings

Make sure you keep your headings in a linear, hierarchical fashion from <h1>..</h1> to <h6>..</h6>.

Paragraphs

There are a lot of options available for paragraphs, from right, left or center aligned.
